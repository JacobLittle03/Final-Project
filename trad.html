Twisted Freddy and Twisted Bonnie from Five Nights at Freddy's: The Twisted Ones. Novel originally released in 2018, character designs by LadyFiszi, and characters by Scott Cawthon.

The Twisted Toys

Twisted Mangle, Twisted Toy Bonnie, and Twisted Toy Chica are fan-designs inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's: The Twisted Ones. Novel originally released in 2018 and character designs inspired by character designs of the Twisted animatronics designed by LadyFiszi and characters by Scott Cawthon.

The Horror
